03 · Web · Luxury Brand

Where exclusivity meets
warmth.

Whimsy Elixir is a private members' club. The brief was to design a full website that felt exclusive without feeling cold — communicating luxury, privacy, and prestige while still inviting the right people in. The site is live.

Role
UI/UX Designer
Deliverables
Full website · Mobile prototype · Design system
Status
Live on Vercel ↗

The Challenge

Luxury is a feeling — and feelings are hard to design.

Most luxury brand websites fail in one of two ways: they're so minimal they feel empty, or they're so ornate they feel dated. The brief for Whimsy Elixir was to thread that needle — exclusive and premium without sacrificing warmth or clarity.

The members' club needed to attract a very specific kind of person: someone who has arrived, expects everything to reflect it, and notices when something is off. That audience reads every detail as a signal.

Design Decisions

Dark backgrounds, gold accents, and typography that earns attention.

The colour palette — near-black backgrounds with 14-karat gold accents — was chosen to mirror the physical membership card (silver-crested, 14-karat real gold). Every design decision was anchored to a real-world object or experience that members would encounter, creating coherence between digital and physical.

The typography scale was deliberately large and confident — the kind of headlines that make you slow down and read. The copy and design had to work together, so I collaborated closely on the language to ensure tone matched visual weight.

The Outcome

A site that feels like the club it represents.

The final product is a multi-page website with a full design system — landing page, membership page, and application flow. The mobile prototype mirrors the desktop experience while adapting layouts for smaller screens without losing visual richness.

The site is live on Vercel. You can experience the design in full at the link above.
