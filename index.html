About Me

Design is about clarity —
not just beauty.

I research before I design. Every project starts with questions: what does the user actually need, where are they getting stuck, and what does the business need from this to work? Only then do I start sketching.

Over the past two years I have worked on live products in fintech and technology — from a payment platform that reached 50+ users in its first weeks, to a booking system, a talent marketplace, and a private members' club experience. Each one taught me something different about how people interact with digital products.

My background is in Food Science (4.30 CGPA, FUTA) — which sounds unrelated, but it gave me a habit of evidence-based thinking that shapes everything I do as a designer.
