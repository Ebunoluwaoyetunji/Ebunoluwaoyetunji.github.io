About Me

I design with curiosity
and a lot of questions.

I'm a self-taught UI/UX designer based in Lagos, Nigeria. My background is in Food Science & Technology — which sounds unrelated, but it gave me something useful: a habit of working from evidence. In that degree, you don't guess. You design an experiment, collect data, analyse it, and then draw conclusions. I do the same thing before I design a screen.

Over the past two years I've worked on live products in fintech, scheduling, luxury branding, and AI-powered talent platforms. Each one taught me something different about how people interact with digital products — and how often the real problem isn't the one you were originally briefed on.

I graduated with a 4.30 CGPA and I'm currently open to remote roles where design is taken seriously and the work actually ships.
