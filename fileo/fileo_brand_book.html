01 — Brand Foundation

Why Fileo exists

The beliefs, purpose, and personality that drive every decision — from the product to the copy to the colour of a button.

"Tax compliance should not require a law degree, an accountant, or three weeks of anxiety."
The founding belief
Mission
Remove the friction between Nigerians and tax compliance
Fileo exists to handle the hard parts of tax filing so that every freelancer, remote worker, and self-employed Nigerian can stay compliant without needing expertise, money, or time they don't have.
Vision
A Nigeria where compliance is effortless
We envision a future where being tax compliant requires no more effort than sending a WhatsApp message. Where the government's systems and individuals' lives are bridged by intelligent, human-centred services.
Purpose
Give people their time, confidence, and peace of mind back
Tax anxiety is real. Fileo's deeper purpose is to remove that specific fear from people's lives — permanently — and replace it with a quiet, settled confidence that they are compliant and protected.
Brand Promise
We handle it. Completely.
Not "we make it easier." Not "we guide you through it." We do it. The user's job is to answer a few questions and approve. Everything else is Fileo's responsibility.
Core Values
🛡️
Trust above all
We handle sensitive financial information. Every design, copy, and product decision must earn and maintain trust. We never overclaim, never hide, never surprise.
✦
Radical clarity
Tax is complex. Fileo is not. We translate every legal and financial concept into language a normal person understands without feeling talked down to.
⚡
Effortless outcomes
The measure of success is not a beautiful interface. It is a filed return, a compliant user, a fear removed. Outcomes over aesthetics, always.
🤝
Human in the loop
AI does the work but humans verify it. A certified tax professional reviews every return. We are not reckless with automation — we are responsible with it.
🌍
Nigerian by design
Built for how Nigerians actually earn — Payoneer, Grey, brand deals, cash, multiple streams. We do not adapt a foreign product. We build for this context.
📈
Privacy as default
User data is used for one purpose: filing their return. It is never sold, shared, or leveraged. Privacy is not a feature we advertise. It is a constraint we obey.
Brand Personality
Calm
Steady, not cold
Fileo doesn't panic, doesn't hype, doesn't add urgency that isn't real. It is the calm, capable voice in a stressful situation.
Competent
Expert, not arrogant
Fileo knows tax law deeply but never shows off. It uses that knowledge to protect the user, not to impress them.
Human
Warm, not robotic
AI powers the service but humans write the words. Every message sounds like a knowledgeable friend, not a chatbot or a legal document.
02 — Brand Strategy

Where Fileo stands

How Fileo is positioned in the market, who it competes with, and what makes it genuinely different.

Positioning Statement
For Nigerian freelancers and remote workers who are newly required to file personal income tax returns but lack the knowledge, documentation, and time to do it alone, Fileo is an AI-native tax filing service that handles the entire process on their behalf — unlike government portals or software tools that still require the user to do the hard work themselves.
Unique Value Proposition
The only service that files for you — not with you
Every competitor in the Nigerian tax space — from Rev360 to Taash to individual accountants — still requires the user to do significant work. Fileo is the only product where the user's job ends at answering questions and pressing approve. The service does everything else.
Competitive Advantage
AI speed, human accountability
Fileo's combination of AI document processing and CITN-certified professional review creates a service that is faster than a human accountant, more trustworthy than pure automation, and more accessible than either. This is the specific combination no competitor currently offers.
Brand Archetype
The Caregiver
with the competence of the Sage
Fileo's primary archetype is the Caregiver — it exists to protect, help, and relieve burden. But it pairs this with the Sage's competence and knowledge. It does not just want to help — it is genuinely capable of doing so. The result is a brand that feels both warm and trustworthy.
🛡️

📖

Knows the law so the user doesn't have to. Uses expertise to simplify, not intimidate.
🤝

Does what it says. No surprises, no hidden steps, no abandoned submissions.

04 — Verbal Identity

How Fileo speaks

Every word Fileo writes is a design decision. The voice is consistent across every surface — from the landing page headline to an error message at 11pm.

Brand Voice Principles
Clear, not clever
We never sacrifice clarity for wit. If someone has to read a sentence twice, we've failed. Plain language is not dumbing down — it's respect.
Your return has been filed successfully.
Your fiscal submission has been successfully processed and transmitted.
Calm, not cold
Warmth doesn't mean exclamation marks and emojis. It means treating the user as a capable adult who happens to be in a stressful situation.
We noticed a transaction we couldn't identify. Help us categorise it.
ERROR: Unrecognised transaction detected. Action required immediately.
Honest, not alarming
We tell the truth about penalties, deadlines, and consequences — but we never use fear as a conversion tool. Facts, not manipulation.
Missing the deadline adds ₦50,000 per month. Filing today stops the clock.
⚠️ WARNING: You could face MASSIVE fines! Act NOW before it's too late!
Confident, not arrogant
We know what we're doing and we say so. But we never talk down to users or make them feel ignorant for not knowing what we know.
We'll handle the calculation. You just need to confirm the numbers look right.
As a tax-compliant service, we utilise advanced computation to determine your liability.

Typography System
Display Font — Cormorant Garamond
Weights: 500, 600
Use: Hero headings, logo, brand moments
Your return,
handled.
Cormorant Garamond brings authority, elegance, and editorial gravitas that most fintech products avoid. This deliberate choice signals that Fileo is not another startup — it is a professional service. The italic variant is particularly powerful for emotional moments and taglines. Rare in Nigerian fintech, immediately distinctive.
Heading Font — Cormorant Garamond
Weights: 600
Use: Section titles, card headings, screen headers
Here's your final return — ready to file.
Using the same typeface for display and heading creates visual hierarchy through size alone, maintaining brand cohesion. The serif heading style gives every screen a premium, considered feel.
Body Font — DM Sans
Weights: 300, 400, 500
Use: Body copy, descriptions, explanations
Tell us how you earned in 2025. We'll ask simple questions — no tax knowledge needed. Most users complete this step in under 3 minutes.
DM Sans is geometric but warm — friendlier than Helvetica, more professional than Poppins, more distinctive than Inter. Its slightly wide letterforms improve readability at small sizes on mobile. An excellent pairing with Cormorant — the contrast between serif elegance and sans warmth mirrors Fileo's personality: authoritative but approachable.
UI / Mono Font — DM Mono
Weights: 400, 500
Use: Currency amounts, codes, HEX values, step numbers
₦412,000  ·  Step 03  ·  #4A7C6F
DM Mono shares DNA with DM Sans — same family, harmonious design. Used exclusively for numbers, amounts, and technical strings. Monospace numerals prevent amount values from shifting visually as digits change.

07 — Moodboard Directions

Three creative directions

Three distinct visual territories explored before arriving at Fileo's final direction. Documented here to show the strategic thinking behind the chosen aesthetic.

Direction 1 — Conservative / Trustworthy
Inspired by established Nigerian financial institutions and legal firms. Deep burgundy, dark mahogany, parchment tones. Traditional serif typography. The user feels they are dealing with an institution that has existed for decades.
Playfair Display · EB Garamond Heavy borders · Dense layout Institutional · Established · Formal
Rejected because: Too old-fashioned for an AI-native product targeting young freelancers. Creates distance rather than warmth. Signals bureaucracy, which is exactly what Fileo is removing.
Direction 2 — Modern AI-Native
Inspired by AI-first products like Notion AI, Linear, and Perplexity. Dark mode first. Electric purple accents. Clean geometric sans-serif. Data visualization aesthetics. Feels cutting-edge and technically capable.
Space Grotesk · Geist Glassmorphism · Gradient meshes Futuristic · Cold · Technical
Rejected because: Purple gradients on dark backgrounds are the most overused aesthetic in AI products globally. Fileo would look identical to a hundred competitors. More critically, it feels cold and impersonal — the opposite of what an anxious user needs.
Chosen Direction
Direction 3 — Premium Fintech
Midnight navy, compliance sage, warm cream. Cormorant Garamond for brand authority, DM Sans for human warmth. Editorial without being cold. Premium without being exclusionary. Designed specifically for the Nigerian freelancer's dual need for trust and accessibility.
Cormorant Garamond · DM Sans Generous whitespace · Warm surfaces Calm · Authoritative · Distinctly Nigerian
Chosen because: It is the only direction that simultaneously achieves trust (navy authority), calm (sage green), warmth (cream surfaces), and distinction (rare in Nigerian fintech). The serif-sans pairing signals both competence and humanity. It is unmistakably Fileo.
